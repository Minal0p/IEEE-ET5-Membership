Membership

Join the IEEE ET5 community and benefit from exclusive resources and events for IEEE members. Be part of a global network of engineers and scientists.

Importance for Students

  • Access to Exclusive Resources: Gain access to IEEE Xplore Digital Library, online courses, and publications that enhance your academic learning.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with faculty, industry professionals, and fellow students through events, workshops, and conferences.
  • Skill Development: Participate in technical workshops, coding competitions, and practical projects to build hands-on skills.
  • Career Guidance: Receive mentorship and insights into various engineering career paths, helping you shape your future.
  • Leadership Roles: Opportunities to take on leadership positions within the student branch, developing organizational and management skills.

Importance for Graduates

  • Professional Networking: Expand your professional network globally, connecting with peers and experts in your field.
  • Continuous Learning: Stay updated with the latest advancements through IEEE's vast collection of research, standards, and continuing education programs.
  • Career Advancement: Access to IEEE Job Site, career development tools, and professional certifications that can boost your career.
  • Industry Recognition: Being an IEEE member demonstrates your commitment to your profession and enhances your credibility.
  • Global Community: Be part of a diverse and inclusive community that fosters innovation and collaboration across borders.

How to Register

Becoming an IEEE member is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to join our global community.

1

Create an IEEE Account

Visit the official IEEE website and create a new account. You'll need to provide your personal details and academic information.

2

Select Membership Type & Branch

Choose your membership type (e.g., Student Member, Professional Member). During the process, select "IEEE ET5 Student Branch" as your affiliated branch.

3

Complete Payment

Proceed to the payment section. You can pay securely online using various methods. Once payment is confirmed, your membership is active!
